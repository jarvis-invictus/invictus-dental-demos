Our Services

Comprehensive Dental Care
Under One Roof

From routine check-ups to advanced restorative procedures, we offer the full spectrum of dental care your family needs — with the personal attention you deserve.

General & Preventive Dentistry

Comprehensive oral examinations, professional cleaning, scaling, polishing, fluoride treatments, and pit & fissure sealants to keep dental problems at bay.

Root Canal Treatment

Painless, precision root canal therapy using rotary endodontics. We save teeth that others might extract — preserving your natural smile for years to come.

Dental Implants

Premium-quality titanium implants for permanent tooth replacement. Full-mouth rehabilitation, single implants, and implant-supported dentures available.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Smile makeovers with porcelain veneers, tooth-coloured bonding, teeth whitening (Zoom & laser), and aesthetic recontouring for the perfect smile.

Paediatric Dentistry

Gentle, child-friendly dental care. From first tooth check-ups to habit-breaking appliances — making dental visits a positive experience for your little ones.

Orthodontics & Braces

Traditional metal braces, ceramic braces, and clear aligners for all ages. Correct misalignment, gaps, and bite issues with time-proven orthodontic methods.

Oral Surgery

Wisdom tooth extraction, impacted teeth removal, jaw cyst treatment, and minor oral surgical procedures — all performed with utmost care and precision.

Gum (Periodontal) Treatment

Scaling, root planing, gum surgery, and management of periodontal disease. We restore gum health and prevent tooth loss caused by gum deterioration.

Crowns, Bridges & Dentures

Precision-crafted porcelain crowns, fixed bridges, and comfortable dentures (complete and partial) to restore function and aesthetics to your smile.

Meet Your Dentist

Dr. Rajesh Mehta

MDS (Prosthodontics), BDS — Principal Dentist & Founder

With over 26 years of dedicated practice, Dr. Rajesh Mehta has built a reputation for meticulous clinical skill and genuine patient care. After completing his BDS from Bharati Vidyapeeth Dental College, Pune, and MDS in Prosthodontics from Government Dental College, Mumbai, he established this clinic in 1998 with a simple philosophy: treat every patient like family.

Dr. Mehta's practice combines traditional values of thorough diagnosis and patient education with modern dental technology. He is known across Sadashiv Peth — and indeed across Pune — for his calm chairside manner, his honest treatment planning, and his insistence on never recommending unnecessary procedures.

  • MDS (Prosthodontics) — Government Dental College, Mumbai
  • BDS — Bharati Vidyapeeth Dental College, Pune (Gold Medalist)
  • Fellow, Indian Prosthodontic Society
  • Member, Indian Dental Association — Pune Branch
  • Advanced training in Implantology (Germany, 2012)
  • Continuing education: 500+ hours of professional development
