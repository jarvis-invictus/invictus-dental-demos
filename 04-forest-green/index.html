Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your visit.

What dental services do you offer?
GreenLeaf Dental Wellness in Viman Nagar offers root canals, metal-free zirconia implants, natural teeth whitening, ceramic crowns, clear aligners, deep cleaning with ozone therapy, and preventive checkups. We use only biocompatible, BPA-free materials for all treatments.
Do you accept dental insurance?
We provide detailed invoices and treatment documentation suitable for insurance reimbursement claims. Most corporate health insurance plans cover basic dental procedures. Please check with your provider for specific coverage. We also accept UPI, cards, and cash payments.
How much does a root canal cost in Pune?
Root canal treatment at GreenLeaf Dental Wellness starts from ₹3,500 using painless rotary endodontics. We use BPA-free biocompatible materials for all fillings and crowns. Dr. Varsha Kulkarni will explain the complete cost breakdown during your free initial consultation.
What are your clinic timings?
GreenLeaf Dental Wellness is open Monday to Saturday from 9:00 AM to 7:00 PM. We are located at Clover Center, Viman Nagar, Pune 411014. Same-day appointments may be available — call or WhatsApp to check.
Do you treat children?
Yes! Our holistic approach extends to children too. We create a calm, anxiety-free environment using aromatherapy and gentle techniques. Dr. Varsha Kulkarni is experienced with young patients and focuses on preventive care, diet counseling, and early intervention. All materials used are 100% safe and BPA-free.
