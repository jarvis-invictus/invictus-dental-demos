Frequently Asked Questions

What dental services do you offer?
Sunshine Dental Studio in Koregaon Park offers root canals, teeth whitening, dental implants, Invisalign clear aligners, kids dentistry, smile makeovers, veneers, and routine scaling & cleaning. Dr. Sunita Joshi handles all cosmetic and restorative procedures personally.
Do you accept dental insurance?
We provide detailed receipts and treatment summaries for insurance reimbursement. Direct insurance billing is not available, but most employer-sponsored mediclaim plans reimburse dental treatments. We also offer 0% EMI options on treatments above ₹10,000.
How much does a root canal cost in Pune?
Root canal treatment at Sunshine Dental Studio in Koregaon Park starts from ₹3,000. Same-day RCT is available using our advanced rotary system. The total cost depends on whether a crown is needed afterwards and the tooth's complexity.
What are your clinic timings?
We are open Monday to Saturday from 9:00 AM to 8:00 PM. Same-day appointments are often available. We are located at Lane 5, Koregaon Park, Pune. You can also WhatsApp us for quick booking.
Do you treat children?
Absolutely! Kids dentistry is one of our specialties. Dr. Sunita Joshi is known for making children comfortable at the dentist with a gentle, friendly approach. We handle preventive care, fillings, fluoride treatments, and early orthodontic assessments for children from age 3 onwards. First visit starts from ₹500.
