Before You Book

Common questions

Do I need to book in advance or can I walk in? +
We strongly recommend booking — we're usually 2–3 days out. Walk-ins are accepted for emergencies (toothaches, broken teeth) but you may wait 30–60 minutes. Call 020-1234-5678 or WhatsApp to check same-day availability.
Is a root canal really single-visit? +
Most straightforward RCTs, yes — about 80% of the cases we see. If there's significant infection or the tooth has complex canals, we may need 2 visits. Dr. Joshi will tell you upfront after looking at your X-ray, not after charging you for the first visit.
Do you accept insurance? +
We don't bill insurance directly, but we provide itemised receipts you can submit for reimbursement. Most corporate health plans cover scaling, fillings, and extractions. Ask your HR department what's covered before booking.
My child is terrified of dentists. Should I still come? +
Yes — and tell us before arriving. Dr. Joshi handles anxious kids regularly. For the first visit we usually just let the child sit in the chair, see the instruments, and get comfortable. No treatment on visit one if they're nervous. It works better long-term.
How much does a checkup cost? +
Consultation is ₹300 (waived if you book a same-day treatment). Scaling starts at ₹800. X-rays ₹250–400 depending on type. We tell you the full cost before starting any procedure — no surprises on the way out.
What dental services do you offer? +
Bright Smile Dental Clinic in Kothrud offers scaling & cleaning, root canal treatment (single-visit rotary RCT), dental implants, Invisalign & braces, kids dentistry for ages 3–16, teeth whitening, fillings, extractions, and full smile makeovers. All treatments are done in-house by Dr. Priya Joshi.
How much does a root canal cost in Pune? +
At Bright Smile Dental Clinic in Kothrud, root canal treatment starts from ₹3,500. Most straightforward RCTs are completed in a single visit of about 90 minutes using painless rotary endodontics. The cost depends on the complexity and the type of crown chosen afterwards.
Do you treat children? +
Yes! We offer dedicated kids dentistry for children aged 3–16 years. Dr. Priya Joshi specializes in fear-free appointments using distraction techniques. For nervous children, the first visit is just about getting comfortable — no treatment is done until they're ready. Kids dentistry starts from ₹500.
