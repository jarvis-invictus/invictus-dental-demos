About the Practice

A clinic built on trust, not advertising

Dr. Rajesh Mehta opened SmileCare Dental in 2010 with a two-chair setup on Baner Road. Fourteen years later, the clinic has grown to five treatment rooms with digital X-rays, a rotary endodontics suite, and a dedicated implantology unit — but the approach hasn't changed.

MDS in Prosthodontics, former consultant at Ruby Hall Clinic, trained at AIIMS Delhi — Dr. Mehta still does every first consultation himself. Over 5,000 patients in Baner, Aundh, and Wakad trust SmileCare as their family dentist.

Most of them found us through a neighbour's recommendation, not a Google ad. That's the way we prefer it.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What dental services do you offer?
SmileCare Dental Clinic in Baner offers root canal treatment, teeth whitening, dental implants, braces & Invisalign aligners, crowns & bridges, scaling & cleaning, and general checkups. All services are available under one roof with Dr. Rajesh Mehta.
Do you accept dental insurance?
We provide detailed itemised invoices for insurance reimbursement. While we do not do direct insurance billing, most corporate mediclaim policies cover dental procedures like fillings, extractions, and scaling. EMI options are also available for expensive treatments.
How much does a root canal cost in Pune?
Root canal treatment at SmileCare Dental in Baner starts from ₹3,500. We use painless rotary endodontics technology. The final cost depends on the tooth location, complexity, and the type of crown selected. A free consultation is available to assess your case.
What are your clinic timings?
SmileCare Dental Clinic is open Monday to Saturday from 10:00 AM to 8:00 PM. We are located at Shop 4, Baner Road, Pune 411045. Walk-ins are welcome, though we recommend booking in advance for specialized treatments.
Do you treat children?
Yes, we offer pediatric dentistry for children of all ages. Our team is trained to handle anxious young patients with a gentle, friendly approach. First consultations for children are free. We cover preventive care, fillings, and orthodontic assessments.
