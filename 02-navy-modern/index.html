✨ What We Do

Gentle care for every member of your family

From your toddler's first tooth to grandpa's new implants — we've got every smile covered with kindness and expertise.

General Dentistry

Routine check-ups, cleanings, and fillings done gently. We take the time to explain everything so you always feel comfortable and in control.

Pediatric Dentistry

Our team is specially trained to work with children. Fun stickers, gentle hands, and patient explanations make every visit a happy one.

Orthodontics & Braces

Straight teeth, confident smiles! Clear aligners and modern braces for teens and adults — with treatment plans that fit your life.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Teeth whitening, veneers, and smile makeovers to help you feel your absolute best. Because you deserve a smile you love to show off.

Root Canal Treatment

Root canals don't have to be scary. Our gentle approach and modern techniques mean you'll barely feel a thing — we promise.

Dental Implants

Missing a tooth? Implants look, feel, and work like the real thing. We'll guide you through every step with care and transparency.

Dr. Priya Sharma

BDS, MDS (Prosthodontics) — SPPU, Pune

Dr. Priya started Warm Smiles with a simple belief: no one should be afraid of the dentist. After years of seeing patients postpone care out of fear, she decided to create a clinic that feels different — one where you're greeted with a smile, treated with patience, and leave feeling genuinely cared for.

She's especially passionate about children's dentistry and has a knack for turning nervous first-timers into kids who actually ask to visit the dentist.
